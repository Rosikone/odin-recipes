Pizza Margherita

Ingredients

  • Flour 200g
  • Water 100ml
  • Brewer's yeast 4g
  • Salt 10g
  • Tomato souce 300g
  • Mozzarella cheese 200g
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Directions

  1. Put the flour on a bowl
  2. Crumble the yeast inside
  3. Pour the water little by little, The right temperature of the water is 25 degrees
  4. When you have added almost all the water, add the salt
